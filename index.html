Stress Management

My Wellness Website

Stress management means understanding pressure, calming the mind, and choosing healthy actions before emotions become too heavy.

Small habits such as deep breathing, good sleep, exercise, and talking to someone trusted can make everyday challenges easier to handle.

Explore Tips

Pause

Taking one slow breath gives the brain a moment to respond calmly instead of reacting quickly.

Plan

A simple routine reduces last-minute pressure and helps keep school, rest, and hobbies balanced.

Share

Talking about feelings with family, friends, or teachers can make problems feel smaller and clearer.
