About the Issue

Understanding Stress Management

Stress is the body and mind's response to pressure. It can happen before exams, competitions, family changes, or any situation that feels difficult.

Stress management is the practice of noticing stress early and using healthy methods to feel steady again. It does not mean avoiding all problems. It means learning how to face them with a calmer mind.

Common Causes of Stress

  • Heavy homework, exams, and deadline pressure.
  • Lack of sleep or too much screen time.
  • Arguments, peer pressure, or feeling misunderstood.
  • Trying to do too many activities without enough rest.
  • Fear of failure or comparing yourself with others.

Effects of Stress

  • Headache, tiredness, stomach discomfort, or body tension.
  • Difficulty concentrating or remembering things.
  • Irritation, sadness, nervousness, or overthinking.
  • Changes in sleep, appetite, and motivation.
  • Reduced confidence when stress is ignored for too long.
